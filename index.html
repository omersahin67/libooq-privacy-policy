ENGLISH Privacy Policy for LiBooq Last updated: June 3, 2026 Overview LiBooq is a reading tracker application developed by Omer Talha Sahin. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. Data We Collect LiBooq stores all your personal data — books, reading sessions, and notes — locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers. We use the following third-party services: Firebase Crashlytics: Collects anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is collected. Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage data such as screen views and app interactions to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected. Data Sharing We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Anonymous crash and analytics data is processed by Google Firebase in accordance with their privacy policy. Data Storage Your books, sessions, and notes are stored on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled. This data is only accessible to you. Children's Privacy LiBooq is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
